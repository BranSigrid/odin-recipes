Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, cheese, and a rich, savory meat sauce. It's a hearty and comforting meal that is perfect for family dinners or special occasions. The layers of cheese melt beautifully, and the sauce is full of flavor, making every bite satisfying and delicious.

This recipe is easy to prepare and can be made ahead of time for a quick dinner on a busy night. The combination of mozzarella, ricotta, and Parmesan cheeses gives this lasagna a creamy and flavorful texture, while the homemade sauce adds depth and richness.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) tomato sauce
  • 1 can (6 oz) tomato paste
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon dried basil
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg, beaten

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large pan, cook the ground beef with the chopped onion and garlic until browned. Drain excess fat.
  4. Add tomato sauce, tomato paste, diced tomatoes, sugar, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper to the pan. Stir well and let it simmer for 30 minutes.
  5. In a separate bowl, mix the ricotta cheese, beaten egg, and half of the Parmesan cheese.
  6. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Place 4 lasagna noodles on top of the sauce.
  7. Spread half of the ricotta mixture over the noodles, then top with a layer of meat sauce, followed by 1/3 of the mozzarella cheese.
  8. Repeat the layers: noodles, ricotta, meat sauce, and mozzarella.
  9. Top the final layer with the remaining noodles, meat sauce, mozzarella, and Parmesan cheese.
  10. Cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
  11. Let the lasagna cool for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
